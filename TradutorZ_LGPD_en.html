Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

The TradutorZ application was developed as a utility tool focused on user privacy. This policy describes how we handle information and permissions on your device.

Privacy Summary: TradutorZ does NOT collect, store, or share personal data, names, emails, or phone numbers from its users.

1. Data Collection and Use

TradutorZ operates under the principle of data minimization. We do not require account creation or login. All text and image translation processing is performed using Google technologies (ML Kit).

2. Device Permissions

To provide translation features, the application requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: Used exclusively to capture images of text, signs, or menus for the OCR (Optical Character Recognition) translation feature. No photos are permanently saved or sent to our servers.
  • Microphone: Used only when the user activates the voice button to convert speech to text. Audio is processed in real-time and is not stored.
  • Storage: Only required for saving translation packages (if the user opts for offline mode) or for loading images from the gallery that the user wishes to translate.

3. Third-Party Services

The application uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you for functional and advertising purposes only:

  • Google Play Services: For basic app functionality on Android.
  • AdMob: To display advertisements. AdMob may collect device advertising identifiers to show relevant ads.
  • Firebase (Google): We use Firebase to manage free usage limits linked to an anonymous device ID and for error analysis (Crashlytics) to ensure app stability.

4. Security

We value your trust in providing access to the camera and microphone, so we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting them. Remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We recommend reviewing this page periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to contact us via the developer email listed on the Google Play Store.
